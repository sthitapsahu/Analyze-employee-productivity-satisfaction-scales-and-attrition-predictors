## 🔍 Root-Cause Attrition & Behavioral Insights
Segmenting employees who exited the corporation versus those successfully retained highlights critical behavioral predictors[span_27](start_span)[span_27](end_span):
*   **The High-Performer Overwork Pattern:** Employees who voluntarily separated from the enterprise possessed a substantially higher performance rating average (**3.31/5.00**) than individuals currently retained (**2.67/5.00**)[span_28](start_span)[span_28](end_span). This shows that topmost talent is separating at a higher rate[span_29](start_span)[span_29](end_span).
*   **The Sentiment Baseline Gap:** Departing personnel showed severe drops in internal satisfaction scores, dropping to an average index of **1.62/5.00**, while retained staff remain high at **3.70/5.00**[span_30](start_span)[span_30](end_span). This correlates directly with project fatigue and long monthly hours (averaging 187.2 hours for exiting staff)[span_31](start_span)[span_31](end_span).
*   **Structural Risk Pockets:** The Engineering segment demonstrates a critical **50.0% attrition rate**, followed closely by Marketing and Operations at **42.9%**[span_32](start_span)[span_32](end_span). Conversely, the Sales division has completely mitigated churn, retaining 100% of its headcount[span_33](start_span)[span_33](end_span).

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations & Actions
1.  **Deploy Targeted Stay-Interviews:** Prioritize structural reviews inside the Engineering and Marketing departments to pinpoint the underlying causes of high employee churn[span_34](start_span)[span_34](end_span).
2.  **Optimize High-Performer Compensation and Well-being:** Establish immediate talent management policies for staff scoring over 3.5/5 on performance scales, addressing their workload levels to mitigate burnout[span_35](start_span)[span_35](end_span).
3.  **Data Analytics Skill Upskilling:** While the Data Analytics department exhibits high project velocity (5.29 projects), its average performance score is low (2.57)[span_36](start_span)[span_36](end_span). Providing target coaching or refining team sprints can boost quality metrics[span_37](start_span)[span_37](end_span).
